The tales use only BasicEnglish words (BASIC ENGLISH: https://en.wiktionary.org/w/index.php?title=Category%3A1000_English_basic_words&pageuntil=daughter#mw-pages).

All the three tales are good for little children.
The first one has 8 triplets
the second one has 10 triplets
the third one has 7 triplets.
